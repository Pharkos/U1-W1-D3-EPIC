Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Carnberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia apperizer topped with sweet caramelized onions , tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 14/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounes brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired trough it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
  2. in a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to icrorporate the last of the flour...
